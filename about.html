About Miguel

Miguel Hugo is currently a fourth-year undergraduate student at University of California, Irvine, double majoring in Informatics and Political Science, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction and International Relations respectively. He is expecting to graduate with both a Bachelor of Science and Bachelor of Arts in Spring 2022.

Miguel finds interest with UI/UX design, coding with Java and Python, and finding ways to engage with and support his community and the communities around him. By developing his tech/design and political background, he aspires to create technological tools to provide avenues of learning political and social justice among underrepresented communities.
